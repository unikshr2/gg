EXPERIENCE

Artiprenuership- 2018

Artiprenuership is a workshop in which we express the current state of our surrounding through pictures. A specific group was formed to work on a project with the theme of pollution in which we should describe how pollution affects our daily life through the pictures. In the end, our pictures were showcased in an exibition where one of my picture was sold for $100.

Photo Walk- 2018,2019

I've been involved with a lot of photographers in a photo walk where I was able to gain new experience and knowledge from other. I have taken part in many such events in 2018 and 2019 where i was able to increase my photography skills.
